Chicken Stroganoff

Credit: Allrecipes / Christina Bailey

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
  • ⅛ cup butter
  • 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Put chicken, butter, and dressing mix in a slow cooker.
  3. Mix together well and cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours.
  4. Add condensed soup and cream cheese. Mix together and cook on High until heated through and warm, about 30 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
